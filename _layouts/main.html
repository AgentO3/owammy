Our Story

We went to Danbury High School together. Graduated the same year but we did not know each other. Fast forward a few years later we met each other through mutual friends, the connection was instant. After 2 years of dating we decided it was time to take the relationship to the next level. With the "go big or go home" attitude we decide not only live together but also to move to Charlottesville VA, "Virginia Is For Lovers" after all. This proved to be one of the best choices we made. The laid-back southern life style allowed us to concentrate on school and our careers.

As a graduation present I promised Tammy a trip to anywhere she wanted. We decided Italy would be the place. I figured this we be the perfect time to finally "pop the big question". I wanted to keep that a surprise, however one night she forced me to spill the beans. I need to rethink my plan; it's no fun if she is going to expect it. Graduation party was coming and a new opportunity. I would have a room full of our friends and family. She wouldn't expect it to happen then, plus it would be nice to share the moment with people we love. So sure enough that night I waited for the perfect opportunity. Just after Kathy Williamson's toast I made a special announcement. As you can probably tell she said "Yes" and everyone rejoiced.
